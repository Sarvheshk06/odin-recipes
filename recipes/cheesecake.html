#1 Classic New York Cheesecake


HomePage

Description

Cheesecake is a popular, rich, and creamy dessert characterized by a base made from crushed cookies, graham crackers, or pastry, topped with a thick, smooth layer of fresh cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 oz) package Cream Cheese, softened to room temperature
  • 1/2 cup Granulated Sugar
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1 cup Heavy Whipping Cream (or 1 container of Cool Whip, thawed)
  • 1 (9-inch) Prepared Graham Cracker Crust

Instructions

  1. Prep: Ensure your cream cheese is soft (let it sit out for 30-60 mins) to avoid lumps.
  2. Mix Cream Cheese: In a large bowl, beat the softened cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla together with a mixer until smooth and creamy.
  3. Add Cream: If using heavy cream, whip it in a separate bowl until stiff peaks form, then gently fold it into the cream cheese mixture. If using Cool Whip, skip whipping and just fold it in.
  4. Fill Crust: Spread the filling evenly into the prepared graham cracker crust.
  5. Chill: Refrigerate for at least 3-4 hours, or until firm.
  6. Serve: Top with fruit, chocolate sauce, or whipped cream if desired.
